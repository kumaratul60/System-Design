HttpOnly Cookies (The Session Guard)

Even if an attacker can run a script, they can't steal your session if your cookies are protected.

Cookies visible to JavaScript:

Why this works:

When a cookie is marked as HttpOnly, the browser hides it from document.cookie. Only the browser can "see" and "send" it to the server. An attacker's script will find nothing to steal.

Observe: In this demo, document.cookie will only show the 'username'. If this were a real server, your 'session_id' would be completely hidden from this page's JavaScript.
